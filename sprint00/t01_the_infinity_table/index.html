The Infinity Stones

Name Color Powers and capabilities Image
Soul Orange The Stone gives the user the ability to manipulate living souls: check for their presence; knock them out of the physical body, and so on. The Stone also contains a pocket dimension called the Soul World, where a person meets someone close to him after using the Stone. What is unique is that to obtain the Stone, it is necessary to perform a ritual of sacrifice, losing someone you love - "Soul for Soul".
Time Green The stone gives the user the ability to manipulate time, control probabilities, and peer into future timelines and possible events.
Space Blue The Stone gives the user the ability to open wormholes and instantly move in space within the Universe. The user of the Space Stone can manipulate space in all senses - move objects (telekinetic actions), change their density and size; use space as a shield; open Black Holes, and so on. The Space Stone emits an infinite amount of energy while in its shell, the Tesseract.
Mind Yellow The stone gives the user the ability to increase intelligence and give other beings sentience. The user of the Mind Stone can manipulate the minds of other beings. The stone is also capable of projecting energy blasts and contains artificial intelligence that can make androids or robotic beings sentient.
Reality Red The Stone's shell, the Ether, is in a liquid state and can be transformed into a crystalline form if necessary. The Stone gives the user the ability to alter reality, create illusions, suck the life force out of other beings, break the laws of physics, and repel any threats it detects.
Power Purple The stone grants the user greatly increased strength and increases the power of anything it is in (such as Ronan's Cosmic Rod or the Infinity Gauntlet). The stone enhances the abilities of other Infinity Stones when used simultaneously. The stone is extremely powerful and unstable, destroying weak beings and causing unexpected explosions and bursts of energy.
