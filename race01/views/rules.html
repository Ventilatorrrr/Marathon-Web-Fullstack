C.A.R.D.

Game Rules

  1. Start of the Game: Each player receives 5 cards. Every card has its own damage value that determines its power in battle.
  2. Gameplay: Players take turns. On their turn, a player may play one card to deal damage to the opponent.
  3. Time Limit: Each move has a time limit. If the player does not act in time, the turn is skipped automatically.
  4. Penalty for Skipping a Turn: Skipping a turn results in penalty damage. The more turns you skip, the worse your position.
  5. Card Damage: Every card has a damage value. Some cards may have special effects â€” boosts, protection, or combos.
  6. Game End: The game ends when one player runs out of cards or loses due to excessive damage. The winner is the one who plays wisely and avoids defeat.

Good luck, and choose your cards wisely!
